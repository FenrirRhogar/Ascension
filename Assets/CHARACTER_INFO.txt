<size=150%><b>HERO CLASSES</b></size>

<b>FIGHTER</b>
<i>"The Unstoppable Force"</i>
<b>Attack:</b> Heavy Greatsword swing.
<b>Ability:</b> Dash — Quick gap closer and dodge.
<b>Ultimate:</b> Berserk — Massively increased attack speed and power.

<b>WIZARD</b>
<i>"The Arcane Master"</i>
<b>Attack:</b> Exploding Fireball.
<b>Ability:</b> Mana Shield — Uses mana to absorb incoming damage.
<b>Ultimate:</b> Meteor Shower — Massive AOE devastation from the sky.

<b>ASSASSIN</b>
<i>"The Shadow Blade"</i>
<b>Attack:</b> Rapid Dual-Dagger strikes.
<b>Ability:</b> Invisibility — Slip into the shadows to become untargetable.
<b>Ultimate:</b> Bladestorm — A whirlwind of deadly steel hitting all nearby foes.

<b>CLERIC</b>
<i>"The Divine Protector"</i>
<b>Attack:</b> Holy Scepter blast.
<b>Ability:</b> Healing Aura — Restores HP to all nearby allies over time.
<b>Ultimate:</b> Divine Light — Full health restoration for the whole team.
