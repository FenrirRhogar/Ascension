# 🎮 Ascension: Class & Controls Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide for your split-screen roguelike adventure. Below you will find the unique powers of each class and how to control them across all hardware setups.

---

## 🛡️ Character Classes

### 🧙‍♂️ The Wizard (The Artillery)
*High-velocity area damage from afar. Physically fragile but magically dominant.*
*   **Attack (Left Click)**: **Fireball** - A fast projectile that travels toward the crosshair.
*   **Ability (Hold Right Click)**: **Continuous Lightning** - A channeled lightning cone that rapidly drains mana and sweeps a wide area for massive group damage.
*   **Ultimate (Q / U / North Button)**: **Meteoric Rain** - Calls down massive explosive meteors from the sky over a wide area.

### ⚔️ The Fighter (The Tank)
*A sturdy frontline warrior who thrives in the thick of battle.*
*   **Attack (Left Click)**: **Slash** - A reliable melee strike aimed via the crosshair.
*   **Ability (Hold Right Click)**: **Iron Whirlwind** - A channeled "Beyblade" spin that drains mana, rotating the player at extreme speed to deal continuous AOE damage.
*   **Ultimate (Q / U / North Button)**: **Heavy Cleave** - A heavy wide-arc swing that deals massive damage to groups.

### 🗡️ The Assassin (The Speedster)
*An agile predator specialized in high-speed "hit and run" tactics.*
*   **Attack (Left Click)**: **Stab** - A rapid, high-precision melee strike that aims dynamically up and down slopes.
*   **Ability (Right Click)**: **Dash** - A tactical burst of speed to close the gap or escape danger.
*   **Ultimate (Q / U / North Button)**: **Ghost Walk** - Instantly perform lethal strikes to all surrounding foes.

### ⛪ The Cleric (The Paladin)
*A resilient support class that balances holy damage with team healing.*
*   **Attack (Left Click)**: **Smite** - A melee strike infused with divine energy.
*   **Ability (Right Click)**: **Divine Pulse** - An AOE pulse that heals yourself and all nearby allies.
*   **Ultimate (Q / U / North Button)**: **Holy Sanctuary** - A massive divine explosion that fully heals allies and smites enemies.

## 📊 Combat & Run Progression

*   **The Ascension Loop**: Finding and touching the Level Gate advances the run to the next stage. Each stage adds more enemies (+3) and scales enemy Health (+50%), Damage (+25%), and Speed (+15%).
*   **Channeled Abilities**: Wizard and Fighter use mana continuously while holding their abilities. Passive mana regen halts while channeling.
*   **Stamina**: Sprinting consumes Stamina. It regenerates automatically when not sprinting.
*   **Ultimate Charge**: Dealing damage fills your Gold bar. At 100%, you can unleash your Ultimate.
*   **Aim-Tracking**: Melee and Magic explicitly use the camera's center pitch/yaw. The visual effects and hit zones perfectly follow your crosshair, enabling easy combat on mountain slopes.
*   **Tactical Camera**: Hold the 'Look Behind' key to instantly flip the camera forward and invert the Y-axis to check your six.
